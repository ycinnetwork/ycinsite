About YCIN

Youth Civic Impact Network (YCIN) was founded to expand access to law, policy, and civic careers for young people from diverse backgrounds.

Mission

We demystify legal and civic careers through workshops, mentorship programs, and hands-on projects so students can build competitive college applications and explore career paths in public service.
