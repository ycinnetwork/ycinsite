Our mission

Youth Civic Impact Network empowers young people to pursue careers in law, public policy, and government with hands-on programs and mentorship.

We partner with schools, law firms, and civic institutions to provide mock trials, policy labs, and college-prep experiences that strengthen applications and career readiness.
