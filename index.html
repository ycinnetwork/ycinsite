Our mission

Youth Civic Impact Network empowers young people to pursue careers in law, public policy, and government through hands-on workshops, mentorship, and practicum-style experiences.

We partner with schools, firms, universities, and civic institutions to create immersive, resume-ready opportunities. Programs are free or low-cost — focused on access, mentorship, and alumni support.

Programs at a glance
